## 🔧 Quick Customization Tips

### Change Colors
In `styles/style.css` (line 8-13):
```css
--primary-color: #3498db;      /* Your main color */
--secondary-color: #1abc9c;    /* Your accent color */
--accent-color: #e74c3c;       /* Your highlight color */
```

### Update Contact Info
In `index.html`, find and replace:
- Phone: `+27 (0) 21 456 7890`
- Email: `info@eclipstravels.co.za`

### Add Your Services
In `index.html`, duplicate service cards and edit:
- Service name
- Icon (search at fontawesome.com)
- Description
- Features
